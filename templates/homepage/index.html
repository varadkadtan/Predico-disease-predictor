About Us

It might have happened so many times that you or someone you know needs immediate medical help, but doctors are not available. Our Disease Prediction System is here to provide end-user support and online consultation. Users can get instant guidance on their health concerns through an intelligent system.

The application is fed with various details and the diseases associated with those details. It allows users to share their disease-related issues and processes user-specific details to check for various illnesses that could be associated with them. We use intelligent data mining techniques to guess the most accurate illness associated with the patient’s details.

Based on the results, patients can contact doctors accordingly for further treatment.
